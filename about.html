About KidsPlot

Your Guide: Anantha Sreeraman

Hello there! I'm Anantha Sreeraman, your friendly neighborhood dad and the brain behind KidsPlot. By day, I'm a mild-mannered software engineer, but by night, I transform into the legendary storyteller – the one who battles bedtime monsters with tales of epic adventures!

Inspiration Behind KidsPlot

Picture this: every night, my son Nivaan Sreeraman challenges me to a duel of wits at bedtime. I must conjure up a story that rivals the excitement of his favorite superhero adventures. But, alas, even superheroes run out of plot twists. And that's where KidsPlot comes in – my secret weapon to never-ending bedtime tales!

My Mission

At KidsPlot, I'm on a mission to conquer bedtime boredom, one hilarious story at a time. My platform is like a superhero sidekick, empowering kids to become the authors of their own bedtime sagas. Join me in the battle against yawns and unleash the creative superhero in your child!


Picture this: a world where bedtime isn't a battle but a burst of uncontrollable laughter. My platform is more than just stories; it's a laughter laboratory, a chuckle chamber, and a joke juggernaut. I'm not just defeating yawns; I'm turning them into yodels of joy!


Join me in the quest to banish serious faces and replace them with smiles that stretch from ear to ear. Together, we'll make bedtime the highlight of the day, with tales so hilarious that even the Sandman can't resist joining the laughter party!
